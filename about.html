About US

Empowering Communities, Conserving Nature

Founded in 2025 by Mr. Bonny Kagaba, the Likana Foundation was created to bridge the gap between underserved communities and the opportunities that sustainable tourism, education, and conservation can offer. We believe that by protecting nature, preserving culture, and empowering people, we can build resilient communities and a better future for all.

Our Mission

At the heart of the Likana Foundation is our mission: to empower underserved communities through education, vocational training, healthcare, and sustainable tourism initiatives.


  • Enhancing individual and family well-being through access to essential resources.
  • Preserving and celebrating cultural heritage within local communities.
  • Promoting responsible tourism that respects traditions and protects the environment.
  • Partnering with local businesses to preserve natural habitats and safeguard wildlife.

Through targeted programs and strategic collaborations, we work hand-in-hand with communities to improve livelihoods and ensure a more resilient, inclusive, and sustainable future.

Our Vision

The Likana Foundation envisions a world where communities thrive through education, economic opportunities, and cultural exchange.


  • To serve as a catalyst for sustainable tourism that uplifts and empowers local populations.
  • To preserve cultural legacies and promote mutual respect between travelers and host communities.
  • To champion environmental stewardship and ensure the conservation of fragile ecosystems.
  • To create a future where prosperity is shared, traditions are honored, and nature is protected.
